Spherical Assembly Game - AutoCAD

Designed a hands-on puzzle game consisting of three interlocking parts that form a complete sphere when assembled correctly. Each piece features unique geometries and subtle cues that challenge spatial reasoning and tactile problem-solving. The goal is to create an engaging and elegant experience that balances difficulty with physical satisfaction.

The design was modeled in CAD with careful attention to tolerances, part interaction, and manufacturability. Inspired by classic mechanical puzzles, the final product is meant to be 3D printed or machined and offers a minimalist aesthetic combined with a rewarding solution.

Top Skills Utilized: 3D Modeling, Tolerance Design, Mechanical Concept Development, Creative Problem Solving
